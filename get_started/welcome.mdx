Adamik offers API tools designed to simplify blockchain integration, providing a faster and more reliable alternative to traditional methods. This portal is your complete guide to using Adamik. It contains all the tools and information you need to navigate the realm of multichain capabilities effectively.

### Key Features

- **Multichain Support**: Seamlessly interact with multiple blockchain networks.
- **Unified API**: Simplifies data retrieval and transaction management.
- **Non Custodial**: You maintain control over your private keys and transactions.

### Why Build with Adamik APIs?

Integrating blockchain functionality can be complex, with challenges such as protocol diversity, infrastructure overhead, and the need for continuous updates. By leveraging Adamik's unified API, you can significantly reduce development time and resource allocation while ensuring robust security measures are in place.

For a deeper understanding of why building on Adamik is advantageous compared to creating your own in-house solution, check out our [Build vs. Buy](./Build-VS-Buy) guide.
